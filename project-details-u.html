Semantic Segmentation with U-Nets

  • Technology Used: Unity, C#
  • Project Date: September 2021
  • Project URL: Github Link
  • Keywords: semantic segmentation, u-net, neural networks, deep learning, satellite images, cnn, transpose convolution

Summary

I implemented the U-Net CNN architecture (Ronneberger et al. 2015) using TensorFlow's Keras API. I trained the model on 2841 satellite images of water bodies and their segmentation masks and acheieved a mean IoU score of 0.748.
