Summary

I developed ImageJ macros to process images of mCNV (Myopic Choroidal Neovascularization), to estabslish OCTA (optical coherence tomography angiography) biomarkers. I leveraged the Thresholding, Segmentation and Analysis tools in ImageJ for quantitative profiling of mCNV.
